Overview

Why foodie goodies?

Every single day, hundreds of tonnes of food are wasted unnecessarily. But it doesn't have to be that way! With just a few clicks of your mouse, you too can be part of the effort that helps fight food wastage right here in Singapore!

Isn't fighting food wastage like super hard? Not at all!
Just follow the steps below:

Step 1:

Find the restaurant you desire

Look through the repository of restaurants on our website and choose the one that appeals to you the most! Detailed information about the restaurant can be found on their profile!

Step 2:

Select your goodies

Pick from 3 sizes of goodie bags: Large, Medium and Small. What is inside is a mystery but larger goodies always yield greater rewards as compared to smaller ones

Step 3:

Collect your Goodies

Come down to the restauraunt and pick up the goodies yourselves! This helps to reduce the impact on the environment as a result of carbon emissions from food delivery services!

But how does it ACTUALLY work?

  1. Simply log on to FoodieGoodies and search for your preferred restaurant.
  2. Confirm your order and reserve your mystery box. To collect, go to the restaurant.
  3. Go home, explore your mystery box and enjoy your meal. You have just saved a box of food from going to waste!
